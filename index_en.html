Majoring in UO Chambara / selective UO illumination

Research

I am conducting research on UO Chambara and the selective illumination of UO (chemical glowsticks). This work represents a cutting-edge fusion of performance technology and materials science. By developing a technique to illuminate only one side of an Ultra Orange (UO) glowstick, the project aims to generate dynamic lighting effects synchronized with movement, enhancing the immersive experience of chambara-style performances. This novel approach pushes the boundaries of glowstick control, opening up new possibilities in live entertainment and interactive choreography. Currently, I am working toward the practical implementation of one-sided UO illumination.
